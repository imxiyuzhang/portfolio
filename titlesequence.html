Why Did I Do It?

I love the movie and have watched it for a few times. The delicacy of it is extremly eye-opening. Its storytelling, color choices, clothing design, audios and architecture addresses the definition of perfection. It inspires me that as a designer, I should be responsible for a large picture, such as the design for the panorama of the hotel from a distance. I aspire to solve problems from the very beginning until the very end instead of only focusing on the digital products. In addition, it encourages me to care about little tiny details too, such as the cake box or a room key. Details affect experiences and they determine the quality of a human-centric solution.
