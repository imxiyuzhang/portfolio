Minditation

Practice your own mindfulness

Overview

This project focuses on providing college students a new option to reduce stress, increase focus and sleep better by listening to binuaral beats therapy while they work on their laptops.

Role

UX Research

UX Design

Prototyping & Testing

Visual Design & Branding

Problem

The difficulty of coping with anxiety and other negative emotions on their own

More and more college students are under pressure due to multiple reasons. 61% of them suffer from anxiety. Other concerns include depression (49 percent), stress (45 percent), family issues (31 percent), academic performance (28 percent) and relationship problems (27 percent). In addition, counselling centers are not avaliable in all schools and a lot of students are too shy to ask for help from counseling services.

Solution

Binaural Beats Therapy

Binaural beats therapy is an emerging form of soundwave therapy in which the right and left ears listen to two slightly different frequency tones yet perceive the tone as one. Proponents of binaural beats therapy liken the effect to that of meditation. Binaural beats therapy is a self-help audio technology that is available to buy worldwide. The purpose for using binaural beats therapy may differ for people. Some need help decreasing their anxiety, while others want to increase their concentration, or deepen their level of meditation.

Users can sign up for premium membership if they would like to have full access to all therapies with no ads and high quality of audios

Takeaways

I would like to explore other forms to present the binaural beats to optimize the user experience. For example, a plug-in in Chrome or a mobile application might bring more flexibility to the students.

In addition, I would want to conduct more user testing if given more time on this project, figuring out how well the therapy helps students regarding to stress reducing, focus increasing and better sleep.
